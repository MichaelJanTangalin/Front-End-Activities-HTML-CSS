About us

Unlad Music Store was founded in November 2021 by Michael Jan Tangalin, the former lead guitarist of Life Than Crowns. The company, which is entirely owned and operated by his friends, is now a retail and sole distributorship of a variety of internationally recognized musical instruments, professional audio equipment, and related accessories.

Adding to the retail music stores are numerous affiliated dealers nationwide that help distribute the products across the Philippines. Driven by a single vision, the highly motivated and empowered team of more than 300 dedicated employees firmly believes in the value and importance of customer satisfaction by offering quality yet reasonably priced products and rendering courteous service at all times.
