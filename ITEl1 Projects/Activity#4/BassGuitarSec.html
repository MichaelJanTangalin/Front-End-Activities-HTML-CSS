  • Finish: White

  • Bridge Pickup: Usually two Single-coils
 The Fender Jazz Bass (often shortened to J-Bass) is the second model of electric bass created by
Leo Fender. It is distinct from the Precision Bass in that its tone is brighter and richer
in the midrange and treble with less emphasis on the fundamental frequency.
The body shape is also different from the Precision Bass, in that the Precision Bass
has a symmetrical lower bout on the body, designed after the Telecaster and Stratocaster
lines of guitars, while the Jazz Bass has an offset lower bout, mimicking the design aesthetic of
the Jaguar and Jazzmaster guitars.
