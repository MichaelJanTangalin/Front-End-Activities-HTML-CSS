Chicken Adobo

By: Michael Jan R. Tangalin

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 30g Canola Oil
  • 24g Crushed Garlic
  • 30g Sliced Onion
  • 1 kg chicken cut ups
  • 30g Vinegar
  • 60g Soy Sauce
  • 240g Water
  • 4g Bay leaves
  • 2g whole Slightly Crushed, Black peppercorns
  • 2 pc Knorr chicken cubes
  • 4g Brown Sugar packed
  • Option: 30g kale or spinach

Kitchen Utensils

  • Spoon & Fork
  • Kitchen Knife
  • Non-stick Frying Pan
  • Spatula

Cooking Process

  1. Heat oil in pan and sauté garlic and onions.
  2. Then add chicken to the pan and sear on all sides, until you have a little browning in the chicken skin.
  3. Pour in vinegar, soy sauce and water.
  4. Add bay leaves, pepper and Knorr Chicken Cubes.
  5. Bring to a boil over high heat then reduce heat to simmer, but do not cover the pan.
  6. Continue to simmer for 10 mins.
  7. Remove chicken pieces from sauce and fry in another pan until nicely browned.
